About Conference

ITSCON-2024

ITSCON-2024 is the Flagship Conference of IEEE Information Theory Society (ITS) Bangalore Chapter ITSCON-202. The theme of this conference is "Exploring the Frontiers of Information Theory" serves as a platform to delve into cutting-edge research and innovative applications within the expansive realm of information theory. This thematic focus aims to push the boundaries of traditional information theory, encouraging exploration into emerging areas, interdisciplinary connections, and novel methodologies.
